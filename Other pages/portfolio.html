Telecommunication Knowledges

Thesis of Open Source 4G LTE Implementation in Smart Lab State Polytechnic of Jakarta

My research on implementing an open-source 4G LTE network using Open5GS and srsRAN is documented in my undergraduate thesis. It includes the network setup, configuration, and performance evaluation using SDR devices such as USRP B210 and LimeSDR.

Conference Proceeding of National Seminar on Vocational Innovation (SNIV)

The findings from my thesis, specifically the performance evaluation of the open-source 4G LTE network, were presented at the National Seminar on Vocational Innovation (SNIV). The presentation covered key performance metrics, including RSRP, Downlink, Uplink, Packet Loss, and the impact of Tracking Area Update (TAU) on network performance.
